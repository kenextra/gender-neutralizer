It’s a success story of fraternal twins, Mr. X and Mr. Y . They went to college. They were freshmen in the college. One of them was jack-of-all-trades, master of none while the other one was number-one-man in the field of mechanics. He was master in his work. Apart from studying he used to spend some man-hours in his desired field. Their mother tongue was French and they were both Frenchmen. Although their father was a Dutchman.
